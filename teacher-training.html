"Your knowledge can change lives - start by understanding the Qur'an the way it was meant to be understood."

Empowering Teachers. Illuminating Hearts.

Al Mauiza Foundation is dedicated to making the understanding of the Qur'an easy, inclusive, and accessible for everyone.

Through its flagship initiative, Understand Qur'an Global (UQG), we are nurturing a new generation of teachers who carry the message of the Qur'an with clarity, compassion, and purpose.

Join this transformative program in Karachi to become a Certified UQG Instructor - equipped to inspire, teach, and impact lives through the divine words of Allah.
